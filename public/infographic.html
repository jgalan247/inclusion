Quick Start Guide

1

Set Up Student Profile

Select up to 3 conditions (e.g., Dyslexia, ADHD, Autism), choose the subject, and pick the appropriate Key Stage (KS1-KS5).

2

Choose Your Task

Select one of the four tabs: Adapt (modify existing resources), Create (build new resources), Quiz (generate assessments), or Convert (export to Word/PDF).

3

Fill In the Details

Follow the wizard steps to provide topic, learning objectives, format preferences, and any specific requirements.

4

Generate & Copy Prompt

Click "Generate Prompt" then copy it to your clipboard. The prompt contains all the instructions the AI needs.

5

Paste into AI (ChatGPT/Copilot)

Open ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, or Claude and paste the prompt. The AI will generate your adapted resource.

6

Convert & Download

Copy the AI's response, go to the Convert tab, paste it in, apply accessibility styling, and download as Word, PDF, PowerPoint, or HTML.

Top Tips for Teachers

  • Use Quick Presets to save time with common condition combinations
  • Select up to 3 conditions to combine adaptations for complex needs
  • The Convert tab can apply dyslexia-friendly fonts automatically
  • Always review AI output before using with students
  • Save your prompts by downloading them as text files for future use
